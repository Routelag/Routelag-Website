Everything you need to know about RouteLag

What exactly does RouteLag do?

RouteLag optimizes your internet connection for gaming by routing your traffic through our global network of servers, reducing ping, packet loss, and jitter. Additionally, our software optimizes your PC settings for maximum performance, boosting FPS and reducing stuttering in games.

Is RouteLag safe to use?

Absolutely! RouteLag is 100% safe and does not modify any system files. It only changes network settings and makes temporary optimizations while the software is running. We do not collect any personal data beyond what's necessary for the service to function.

Will RouteLag work with any game?

Yes! RouteLag works with all online games. Our optimization techniques are game-agnostic and focus on improving your network connection and system performance, which benefits any game you play.

How much ping reduction can I expect?

Results vary depending on your location, internet provider, and the game servers you connect to. Most users see a 20-70% reduction in ping, with some experiencing even greater improvements. Our optimization techniques are particularly effective if you're experiencing routing issues with your ISP.

Do I need a powerful PC for RouteLag to work?

Not at all! RouteLag is designed to work on any PC, from high-end gaming rigs to older systems. In fact, users with older or less powerful PCs often see the most dramatic improvements as our optimization techniques help maximize the available resources.
